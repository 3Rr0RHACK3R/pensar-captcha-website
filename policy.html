1. The Lowdown on Your Data (What We Collect)

Alright, listen up, fam! Since your super-secret authenticator app, PensarIMAGE, lives *only* on your personal computer (no cloud, no funny business, just pure local vibes), we're not actually collecting your personal data. Like, zero. Nada. Zilch. Your secret keys stay on your machine, locked down tighter than your crush's DMs.

What your app *does* store (locally): Your chosen usernames and their hashed passwords (super secure, thanks to bcrypt!), and your TOTP secret keys. But again, this is all on *your* device, not ours. We can't see it, access it, or accidentally leak it. Phew!

3. Your Rights (You're the Boss!)

Because your data is on your device, you have full control, you absolute monarch!

  • Access: You can literally open the `user_data` folder and see your hashed usernames (though the secrets are in separate files for each user, you savvy hacker!).
  • Rectification: You can change your password or delete accounts within the app.
  • Erasure: Delete the app, delete the `user_data` folder, and poof! Your data is gone from your machine. We can't help you with that, because, well, we don't have it.

4. Security (We're Not Messing Around)

While we don't handle your data, we've built the app with some decent local security in mind:

  • Password Hashing: Your login passwords are not stored in plain text. They're hashed using `bcrypt`, which is like putting them through a digital shredder and then setting the shreds on fire.
  • Separate User Data: Each user gets their own private little corner in the `user_data` directory, so your secrets aren't mingling with your roomie's.

However, remember, this is a local app. Your device's security is paramount. If your computer gets compromised, well, that's a whole different level of "oopsie." Keep your OS updated and use strong passwords!
